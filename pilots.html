Pilots deployed using Agent platform

1. Safety reminder - Medication expiration alert
2. Reinforcing and monitoring clinical protocol - Extubation risk screen reminder
3. Nursing task efficiency and awareness - New STAT meds and infusion orders

#1 \\ Effectively reminded residents to reorder medications that would otherwise have been forgotten.
3 cases were caught on 3 medicine services over two weeks, with no doses missed.
Revealed opportunity for improvement of choice architecture and workflow integration of existing EMR alert.

    -->>--    


#2 \\ Brought adherence to protocol from 43% to 93%.
Alert enabled real-time monitoring by MICU director, and reinforcement at multiple
care levels perpetuated a culture of awareness of protocol.
[PI = post-intubation order set, which has embedded extubation risk screen]

#3 \\ Revealed "task list" functionality of text message threads,
and utility of notifications for nurses at times when they were unable to leave a patient room.
Alert fatigue was minimal due to specificity of alerts, even when information
was already known or previously seen on EMR.
